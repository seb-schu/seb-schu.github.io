Biography

Hi, my name is Sebastian and I’m a Social Scientist by training. My scientific interest lies in the combination of social science questions, especially in the field of poverty, migration and education, and the scientific field of Data Science.

For this reason I focused on Methods of Social Science in my Master studies and gained a lot of knowledge in statistics and R that I gradually expanded by answering questions or solving problems at work and completing online courses.
